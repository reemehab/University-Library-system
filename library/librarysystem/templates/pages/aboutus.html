ABOUT US

The materials collected by the University Library make it possible to discover, study, and enjoy cultural treasures and significant historical documents including books, manuscripts, maps, newspapers, journals, prints and photographs, sound recordings, and films. Content in the University Library was selected by partner institutions in accordance with guidelines set by the University Library Content Selection Committee. Content was chosen for its cultural and historical importance, with due regard to recognition of the achievements of all countries and cultures over a wide range of time periods. Books, manuscripts, maps, and other primary materials on the site are presented in their original languages. More than 100 languages are represented in the University Library collection, including many lesser known and endangered languages.
